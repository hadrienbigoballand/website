About Me

I am a PhD student in Applied Mathematics collaborating with INRIA Paris and MyFit Solutions. My research focuses on leveraging advanced mathematical tools for automatic 3D measurements from smartphones, addressing real-world challenges, especially in the medical field.

My work lies at the intersection of geometry, shape analysis, optimal transport, and computer science. I aim to modernize and democratize measurement techniques in healthcare.
