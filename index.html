Hi, I'm Hadrien.

I am currently pursuring a PhD at MyFit Solutions, in the HEKA team of INRIA Paris and under the supervision of Professor Stéphanie Allassonnière, Dr. Jean Feydy and Jeremy Adoux for the last

I am also contributing to scikit-shapes, an open-source Python library for shape analysis and statistical learning on geometric data, designed to facilitate research and applications in computational anatomy, morphometrics, and related fields.

Additionally, I have been serving as a Teaching Assistant of Probability Theory at Lyon 2 Lumière University, working in collaboration with Professor Stephane Chretien for three years and Zied Gharbi for one year.
